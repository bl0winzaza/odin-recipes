The Best Tomato Sandwich!

I first made this sandwich because my dumb neighbor was letting the tomatoes and basil from her summer garden rot on her countertop.

The outrage I felt witnessing this travesty compelled me to sneak into her home and make myself a delicous tomato toast for lunch.

Ingredients:

  • Bread- use whatever toasts best, I like wholegrain sandwich bread
  • Mayonaise
  • Garlic- fresh, powdered or roasted- use what u got!
  • Fresh basil baby!
  • Big, sexxy, red and juicy tomatoes (must be in season)

Assembly:

  1. Toast the bread until it is very crunchy
  2. Let the toast dry for a minute or two to avoid sogginess
  3. Slice your tomato in nice thick slices. You can use a paper towel to dab away excess juice if you're worried about the sandwich being too juciy
  4. Like all good sandwiches slap a crap ton of mayo on it
  5. Layer sliced tomatoes
  6. Top with fresh basil and whatever garlic you are using
