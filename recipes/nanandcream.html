Nan-and-Cream Toast!

This toast. This toast is the greatest thing I have ever created.

I know that isn't exactly an impressive claim, especially if you're reading this on my first-ever webpage, but it's the truth so I thought I'd mention it.

A salty-sweet, crispy, chewy and creamy dessert toast that I cobbled together one afternoon as a hungry child left alone in my mother's ill-stocked kitchen.

Store-bought bread (either white or wholegrain) is the best kind for this toast, it has the best texture.

Ingredients:

  • Bread, regular or thin-sliced
  • Half of a well-ripened bannana
  • Your favorite cream cheese
  • Honey- the more liquid-y for drizzling the better

Assembly:

  1. Toast bread until crispy, yet still chewy in the center
  2. Slice your bannana into cute rounds. Not ugly strips
  3. Spread a thicc layer of cream cheese
  4. Layer the banana slices on top
  5. Add a drizzle of honey all over the whole thang
  6. Enjoy that shit
