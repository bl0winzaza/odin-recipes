Zaza's Onion Sammie!

A simple yet extremly satisfying masterpiece, Zaza's onion sandwich requires fresh ingredients and a love of raw onions

  • Fresh baked bread
  • Home made mayonaise (never storebought!)
  • Thinly sliced red onion
  • Ketchup with sugar

After gathering and preparing your ingredients, assembly is simple:

  1. Slather your bread with a generous amount of mayonaise
  2. Lay as many onion slices as you prefer on the mayo
  3. Dot the open-face of your sandwich with sweet sweet ketchup
  4. Enjoy!
